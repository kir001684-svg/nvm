About Us

We are a grassroots NGO committed to social upliftment through education, women's empowerment, and health awareness.

Who We Are

Nehru Vikash Manav Samity is a community-driven organization working tirelessly to uplift underprivileged sections of society. Through education, healthcare initiatives, and empowerment programs, we bring real change at the grassroots level.

🎯 Our Mission

To foster inclusive development by ensuring access to education, healthcare, and empowerment opportunities for all.

🌟 Our Vision

To create a just and equitable society where every individual lives with dignity, health, and purpose.
