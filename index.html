Our Vision

At Nehru Vikash Manav Samity, we envision a society where every child has access to quality education, women are empowered to lead, and healthcare is a basic right for all. Through dedicated programs and awareness drives, we strive to build inclusive and healthy communities.

Our Focus Areas

📘 Education

Providing education to underprivileged children and supporting learning initiatives in rural areas.

👩‍⚕️ Health Awareness

Conducting health camps and spreading awareness on hygiene, nutrition, and preventive care.

👩‍💼 Women Empowerment

Empowering women through skill training, self-help groups, and legal literacy programs.
